# Launch copy — driving petitioners to the page

Voice: ritual, restrained, no hype, no emoji-spam. You never *sell* — the Craft does
not solicit. You state what it is and let the right person knock. Lead with the
free founding cohort + its scarcity (it closes). Never frame it as mental-health
treatment — this is self-mastery / a society of practice.

Link everything to the petition page (`/petition/`, or your custom domain root).

---

## 1 — The summons (X / Threads / IG caption)
> Most men die rough-hewn — the same stone they were quarried as.
>
> The Ashlar Protocol is a private society for the shaping of the self. Three degrees. Daily work. Entry by petition.
>
> The founding cohort is forming, and it's free. It will not stay either.
>
> Knock: [your-domain]

## 2 — Founding cohort announcement (longer post / email / Discord)
> **The Ashlar Protocol — the door is open, briefly.**
>
> A society built on one old idea: every stone leaves the quarry rough, and the whole art is making it true. We apply that art to a life — through degrees you earn by the work, not by paying, and a daily practice that never punishes you for a missed day.
>
> We're raising the **founding cohort** now. Founders are initiated **free**, and keep their place free when pricing opens to everyone after. The cohort is limited and closes once we reach our first density.
>
> You don't get invited. You petition. Tell us who you are and why you seek the Craft: [your-domain]

## 3 — One line (DM / bio / reply)
> A private society for the shaping of the self. Free founding cohort, by petition, closing soon → [your-domain]

## 4 — Who it's for (targeted post)
> This isn't for everyone, and it's not meant to be.
>
> It's for the person who's tired of drifting — who wants structure, discipline, and a way to become deliberate, worked at a little every day. If that's you, the founding cohort is open and free. Petition: [your-domain]

## 5 — Story / short-form video hook (spoken)
> "There's a stonemason's idea I can't stop thinking about. Every block leaves the quarry rough. The mason's entire craft is making it *true* — square, level, plumb — until it can hold a wall. What if you did that to yourself, a little every day? That's the whole idea behind a society I'm opening the doors to. It's called the Ashlar Protocol. Founding members get in free. Link's below — but you have to ask to join."

---

### Notes
- **Scarcity is real, so keep it real.** "Closes at first density" — pick a founder cap (e.g. 100) and hold to it, or the urgency rings hollow.
- **Don't overpromise the lodge yet.** Say "brotherhood, once your lodge is formed" — the social layer is Phase 1. Selling community you don't have yet is how trust dies.
- **Track the source.** Add `?from=x`, `?from=ig` etc. to the link per channel so you learn which message pulls petitions.
