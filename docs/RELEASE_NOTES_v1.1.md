# The Ashlar Protocol — v1.1 "The Temple"

A research-grounded companion for building a steadier, more deliberate life — themed on the craft of
the stonemason (the rough ashlar smoothed toward the perfect). Fully **on-device**: no account, no
servers, no internet permission, nothing leaves your phone.

## The headline: The Temple

A long-arc journey of **50 courses**, each teaching a real, research-cited practice. You earn
**wages** (corn, wine, and oil) by doing small daily and weekly challenges, and **lay** them to raise
the courses of your Temple — spending that *builds* rather than depletes (spent, but never lost).

- **Daily / weekly / monthly** — an optional daily menu of behavioral-activation micro-actions, a
  value-aligned weekly challenge, and a monthly reflection folded into the mirror.
- **50 courses across three degrees** — Apprentice → Fellowcraft → Master Mason, from showing up and
  self-compassion through connection, urge-surfing, and generativity, up to the summit.
- **Adorn your Temple** — buy a finish (Sandstone, Marble, Verdigris, Rose, Lapis) that recolours the
  rising courses. Yours for good; free to switch.
- **No points, no coins, no gambling loops** — the evidence says an extrinsic currency adds nothing to
  outcomes and can undermine the motivation that sustains change, so the whole thing runs on
  build-your-Temple, never a reward economy.

## Also in this release (the craft beneath the Temple)

- **The kind streak** — "tending the stone" that only ever grows; a missed day never resets it.
- **Working tools** — the Gauge (activity scheduling), the Plumb (thought records), the Cornerstone
  (environment design), if-then Practices, the Rough Edge (a forgiving anti-relapse track).
- **The West Gate** — gentle prompts to turn toward the people you trust.
- **What the Stone Remembers** — a private, on-device mirror that reflects your own data back as
  plain facts and carefully hedged noticings (never a diagnosis, never a claim of cause).
- **Always-on support** — the crisis pathway sits above everything and is never gated behind progress.

## Under the hood
- 100% on-device, no INTERNET permission, no telemetry.
- Every user-facing line is swept through safety gates (no harmful imagery, no willpower/grit framing).
- 206 automated tests; the Temple loop device-verified.
